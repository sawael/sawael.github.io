About me.

I'm Al-Saeed Wael, a seasoned frontend developer and graphic designer with over five years of experience in creating visually stunning and functionally robust digital experiences. My expertise spans the full spectrum of Adobe applications, including Photoshop, Illustrator, and XD, allowing me to craft captivating designs and seamless user interfaces.

In addition to my design skills, I am proficient in frontend web development, utilizing languages such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to build responsive, high-performance websites and applications. My approach combines creativity with technical precision, ensuring that every project not only meets but exceeds client expectations.

I also founded ONE PLAINS, a freelance company dedicated to helping small businesses build their brand identity through web development, graphic design, and comprehensive branding guidelines. At ONE PLAINS, we specialize in crafting unique visual and digital solutions, utilizing typefaces like 'Adele Sans Devanagari Heavy' and 'Arial Black' to create bold and effective brand identities.

Explore my portfolio to see a curated selection of my work, showcasing my ability to blend design and code to deliver exceptional results. Whether you're looking for a striking brand identity or a sophisticated web solution, I'm here to help you bring your vision to life.
